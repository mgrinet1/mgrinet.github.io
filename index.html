Welcome!

You've reached my online portfolio.

I am a Biomedical Engineer with a passion for Machine Learning and Image Processing and building solutions that translate raw data into knowledge and information. I have 8+ years of experience in academic R&D, and 6+ years of experience as a Data Science and Machine Learning consultant. I've worked on a variety of problems involving Georeferenced data, Timeseries data, NLP, Medical Images (DICOM) and Computer Vision. I've worked on complex visualization solutions for Telecommunications data, Recommendation systems for B2B retail, Object Recognition and Tracking for the medical sector, and Mechanical Failure Forecasting for predictive engine maintenance. I've been developed scalable data-driven NLP and Timeseries solutions on the cloud for a client in the financial sector.
